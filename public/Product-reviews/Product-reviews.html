Care & Maintenance Guide

Dry Cleaning

Always dry clean only. Do not attempt to wash at home as it may damage the delicate embroidery.

Ironing

Iron on low heat setting. Place a cotton cloth between the iron and saree to prevent direct heat.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place. Wrap in muslin cloth to allow fabric to breathe. Avoid plastic covers.

Air Out

Air out the saree periodically to prevent moisture buildup and maintain freshness.
